The Next Evolution of Sophistication Among Downtown Hotels in Miami

Downtown Miami Hotels: Kimpton EPIC Hotel Gorgeously renovated and ready to welcome you. Kimpton EPIC Hotel is your exquisite base camp for exploring Miami. Our glamorous, flirty city loves showing off her outdoorsy side. Miami’s tropical climate inspires alfresco activities day and night, all year long. Relax in a spacious cabana by our two sky-high pools, enjoy dinner and drinks alfresco at our Area 31 restaurant, walk barefoot on nearby hidden beaches, go kayaking and boating, or visit open-air attractions like artsy Wynwood. It’s time to experience this glimmering paradise in exhilarating new ways.

VISITING THE ROOFTOP

Non-residents are welcome to visit our rooftop bar & terrace which are open daily from 12:30 to 23:00. Drinks and light snacks are available and no reservation is required. But please note that the bar gets very busy & we have limited capacity so arrive early to be sure of entry. If you want to follow drinks by lunch or dinner on the rooftop, a reservation is required for our restaurant. We’ve also got a beautifully curated boutique that's worth a visit.
